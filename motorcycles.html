Motorcycles

Motorcycles are two-wheeled motor vehicles known for their agility and efficiency. They come in many styles like cruisers, sportbikes, and dual-sport bikes.

Quick facts

  • Typical seating: 1–2 people
  • Fuel type: mostly petrol (some electric models exist)
  • Common types: cruiser, sport, touring, dual-sport
